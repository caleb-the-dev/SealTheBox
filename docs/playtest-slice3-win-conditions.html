How to test: Press T (or DEV button) → Go to Box → → pick the box name. Play a full match, not just 1–2 rounds — WIN boxes only reveal themselves at decision time.

Escalating Threshold tip: Use DEV → Force Round X to jump to round 3 or 4 and feel the tighter threshold without grinding through the whole match first.

What you're testing: WIN boxes change what counts as winning, not how dice work. Ask yourself: did the win condition change your strategy? Did it create real tension — or just frustration?

KEY QUESTIONS THIS SLICE IS TESTING

  1. High-stakes or unfair? (Crit Only) — Removing the threshold exit should raise stakes. Does it feel like a meaningful challenge, or did it feel like a punishment with no way out?
  2. Panic vs. urgency? (Escalating Threshold) — Round 1 threshold is generous. Later rounds get tight. Does that arc feel tense and fun, or just stressful and arbitrary?
  3. Did you change your strategy? — Did knowing the win condition push you to play differently (bigger seals early, no threshold coasting, etc.)?
  4. Win condition clarity? — Could you tell from the UI what the rule was? Did the threshold label update in a way that made sense, or did something feel broken?
